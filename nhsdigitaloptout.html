Therefore I have made this page specifically to make this easy for the everyday guy or gal to do. Please follow the steps below...


Option One - By Post

1) Print BOTH these forms Type 1 Opt-Out and NHSD

2) Fill and send both forms to the below address...

   National Data Opt Out
   Contact Centre
   NHS Digital
   HM Government
   7 and 8 Wellington Place
   Leeds
   LS1 4AP

Option Two - Smartphone

1) Download and install the signing app "Adobe Fill & Sign" (no need to register)

2) Save both NHS forms to your mobile device Type 1 Opt-Out Form and NHSD form

3) Open one form at a time in "Adobe Fill & Sign" and start filling out the forms. When you have done this for both forms send them off through the app to this email address enquiries@nhsdigital.nhs.uk


Option Three - Desktop PC

1) Open web browser and navigate to PDFescape and click on "Free Online", left hand corner.

2) Select the third option down "Load PDF from Internet" and copy and paste the following URL Type 1 Opt-Out Form, then click "Load"

3) Fill the form out. You can even put your signiture on using the "freeform" tool on the left hand side. Once you have finished, click on the "save and download" icon on the left.

4) Repeat step 3 using the second URL NHSD form and email both forms to enquiries@nhsdigital.nhs.uk
